Who am I?

I'm an experienced telecommunications and computer science engineer with a focus on Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN), 5G, and advanced network processing. Currently pursuing a PhD at UC3M, I have contributed to projects like Predict-6G and Hexa-X-II, specializing in multi-domain 6G architectures, predictive models, and OpenAPI specifications.

What I do?

I develop deterministic network solutions, predictive AI models, and perform large-scale data analysis. My expertise includes deploying TSN solutions, implementing OpenAPI specs, and handling network performance tasks using state-of-the-art tools like OpenAirInterface and Amarisoft.
